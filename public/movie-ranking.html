The Best Movie According to Ula

My top 3 movies off all-time

Secretariat

The film Secretariat is about the true story of a remarkable racehorse who won the Triple Crown in 1973. It follows the journey of his owner, Penny Chenery, as she overcomes obstacles to lead him to victory.

Sing

The film Sing is about a group of animals who enter a singing competition to pursue their dreams. It focuses on their personal struggles, friendships, and the joy of music.

McFarland

The film McFarland, USA is about a high school cross-country team from a small town that overcomes challenges to achieve success. It follows their coach and the runners as they build determination, teamwork, and hope for a better future.
